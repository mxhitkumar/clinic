    The Evolution of Excellence: K.L. Dental

    What began as a modest dental clinic in 2015 has grown into one of the most advanced and trusted dental centers in Charkhi Dadri.

    • Founded by Dr. Saurabh Singhal after his training at DAV Dental College, Yamuna Nagar, with a strong focus on ethical and patient-first care.
    • Early adoption of Rotary Endodontics in 2016, making root canal treatments faster, more precise, and significantly more comfortable.
    • Introduction of Digital X-Ray (RVG) and advanced Implantology in 2018, offering accurate diagnosis and permanent tooth replacement solutions.

    As patient trust grew, K.L. Dental expanded its infrastructure with multiple dental chairs, reduced waiting times, and added specialized orthodontic services. Today, the clinic serves as a complete dental solution—from routine cleanings to complex full-mouth rehabilitations.

    With over 15,000 happy patients, 7,000+ successful root canals and crowns, and advanced multi-specialty facilities, K.L. Dental continues to set new standards in modern dentistry. Here, every patient becomes part of a decade-long legacy of care, innovation, and excellence.

    Education & Training

    Strong academic roots built on clinical excellence.

    Dr. Singhal completed his BDS in 2013 from D.A.V. Centenary Dental College, Yamuna Nagar. After graduation, he spent two intensive years working at reputed clinics, gaining hands-on experience in complex dental procedures and patient management.

    Clinical Experience

    Since establishing his practice in 2015, Dr. Singhal has treated more than 15,000 patients. His expertise includes over 7,000 successful root canal treatments and 7,000+ crowns and bridges, helping patients restore both function and confidence.
